Our Story

Jay Joshi started early in the music space, forming Ahmedabad’s first band in 2008 at the age of 16 and leading it actively until 2010. He later moved to Mumbai to pursue film school, completing his sound engineering studies while continuing to perform as a solo artist and bassist, building a strong technical and production-driven approach to music.

Tejas Vora began his musical journey as a vocalist, performing at live shows and competitions during his academic years. In 2011, he expanded into composition and lyric writing at ISKCON, New Vidyanagar, Anand — developing a strong foundation in melody, poetry, and emotional storytelling through music.

Both artists eventually began working together creatively, combining lyrical sensibility with production depth and cinematic sound.

In 2015, they released their first track under Manzilll Production, marking the beginning of a shared creative journey focused on original compositions and evolving musical expression.
